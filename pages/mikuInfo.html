Hatsune Miku

Hatsune Miku (初音ミク), also known as CV01, is a Japanese VOCALOID developed and distributed by Crypton Future Media, Inc. She debuted in August 2007 as part of the VOCALOID2 engine and became the first character in the Character Vocal Series. Miku was the seventh VOCALOID created overall and the second to be released for the VOCALOID2 platform. Her voice is provided by Japanese voice actress Saki Fujita (藤田咲, Fujita Saki).
